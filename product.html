THE VOID SERIES

The Void Series No. 04

An exploration of negative space and the transient nature of shadow. This piece is hand-carved from volcanic clay and finished with a unique metallic reduction glaze.

DIMENSIONS

24cm x 24cm x 32cm

MATERIAL

Volcanic Clay, Gold Leaf

ORIGIN

Kyoto Studio

WEIGHT

4.2 kg

The Alchemist's Touch

Each piece in The Void Series is born from twenty-four hours of continuous kiln firing. The artist, Elias Thorne, monitors the reduction atmosphere to ensure the metallic luster develops its characteristic depth.

The interior is gilded with 24k gold leaf, creating a "halo" effect when light strikes the inner rim, symbolizing the spark of life within the silence of the form.
