Master Ceramist

Hiroshi Nakamura

A life dedicated to the dialogue between clay and flame. Based in the remote hills of Mashiko, Nakamura-san crafts stoneware that captures the fleeting moment of transformation within the kiln.

The Philosophy of Absence

Wabi-Sabi in Motion

Nakamura's work is rooted in the belief that perfection is found in the accidental. His "Anagama" kiln is fired continuously for seven days, using red pine wood to create natural ash glazes. He does not control the outcome; he merely facilitates the meeting of elements.

"The clay has its own memory. My hands only remind it of where it wants to go. The fire then seals that memory into stone. I am the silent curator of this conversation."

Nakamura Hiroshi, 2023
